This website is a personal portfolio. I don't sell anything, I don't run ads, and I don't track you with cookies. Below you'll find the details on what data is processed and why.

Hosting
This site is hosted on Vercel (Vercel Inc., San Francisco, USA). When you visit a page, Vercel's servers automatically receive technical data such as your IP address, browser type, operating system, and the page you requested. This is necessary to deliver the website to you. Vercel may store server logs briefly for security and performance purposes. More info: vercel.com/legal/privacy-policy.

Analytics
I use Vercel Web Analytics and Speed Insights to understand how many people visit my site and how fast it loads. These tools are privacy-friendly: they don't use cookies and don't track you across websites. They collect anonymized, aggregated data (e.g. page views, country, device type). No personal profiles are created.

Language preference
When you switch the language (EN/DE), your choice is saved in your browser's local storage. This is a small piece of data stored only on your device — it's never sent to any server. You can clear it anytime via your browser settings.

External links
This site contains links to external websites (e.g. LinkedIn, Google Maps). When you click on these links, you leave my site and the respective provider's privacy policy applies. I have no control over how these third parties handle your data.

No cookies
This website does not set any cookies.

No contact forms
There are no forms on this site. If you contact me via email, your message and email address are only used to respond to your inquiry.

Your rights
Under applicable data protection law (GDPR / Swiss FADP), you have the right to access, correct, delete, or restrict the processing of your personal data. You can also object to processing or request data portability. Just send me an email at rafael.balle@outlook.de.
